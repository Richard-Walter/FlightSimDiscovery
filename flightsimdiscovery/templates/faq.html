Frequently Asked Questions

General
What is Flight Sim Discovery?
Flight Sim Discovery is a free, community-based web application that allows you to find points of interest (POI's) to visit and explore in the world of flight simulation.
What is a POI?
POI is an acronym for "Point of Interest". A POI is a place in the world that you can visit and explore in your flight simulator, or the real world!
Points of Interest (POI)
How do I report an issue with a POI?
You need to be logged in to report a POI. Click on the POI you want to report. If it hasn't been reported before, a yellow flag icon will appear at the bottom left of the POI information window. Click this icon, enter a reason and submit. The administrator will review all reported POIs.
How do I create a POI?

You need to be logged in to create a POI. The easiest way is to 'right-click' at the exact location on the map, and click 'create POI at this location' button. If you already have coordinates of a POI, you can create a POI from the navigation bar and enter in the coordinates.

How do I find and edit POI's that Ive created?
POIs that you have created are represented by a black user icon on the map. Click on your POI then the update icon in the botton-right corner of the POI information window. Alternatively, you can go to Account -> 'My Points of Interest'
How do I mark a POI as 'visited'?
You need to be logged in to do this. On the map, click on the POI. In the top-right corner of the POI information window, click on the black box icon to mark the POI as 'visited'. Please note that POIs are automatically marked as 'visited' for your convenience whenever you export a flight plan. You can see which POIs you have visited in Account -> 'My Points of Interest'
How do I mark a POI as a 'favorite' so I can explore it later?
You need to be logged in to do this. On the map, click on the POI. In the top-right corner of the POI information window, click on the red heart icon to mark the POI as a favorite. You can see which POIs you have favorited in Account -> 'My Points of Interest'
Search
How come when I search for a particular country, it includes POI's from other countries as well?
This is intentional because in most cases you want to see POIs from surrounding countries as well. Searching by country just affects the location and zoom level on a map, making it easier to find POI's for that country. The same applies for searching by region.
After a search, how can I go back and see the map of the whole world?
Easiest way is using the scroll wheel on the mouse to zoom the map out. You can also change the country search to 'Country' and search again, click on the 'Flight Sim Discovery' logo at the top-left corner of the page.
Flight Plan
I created a flight plan, but I couldn't enter the departure and arrival airports?
The departure and arrival airports are determined automatically based on the proximity to the first and last POI. This was done to make exploring places as easy and quick as possible. If you wish, you can edit the flight plan within your flight simulator, or use many of the free 3rd party fligh planning tools available, such as Little Navmap
